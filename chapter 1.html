Chicken Adobo

Chicken Adobo is a classic Filipino dish of chicken and vegetables cooked in soy sauce and vinegar. Tangy, salty and rich, and it goes perfectly with a large platter of garlic fried rice.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar (white or cane vinegar)
  • 1 head garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2-3 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon black peppercorns
  • 1 tablespoon sugar (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Methods:

  1. In a bowl, combine the chicken, soy sauce, vinegar, minced garlic, and black peppercorns. Marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat the cooking oil in a large pot over medium heat. Saut√© the sliced onion until translucent.
  3. Add the marinated chicken (reserve the marinade) and cook until lightly browned.
  4. Pour in the reserved marinade, add bay leaves, and bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 30-40 minutes, or until the chicken is tender.
  6. If desired, add sugar to balance the flavors and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice.
